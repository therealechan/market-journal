Today's gap-down recovery is a **constructive signal** — real buyers are present at the support zone. However, single-day behavior is insufficient to confirm capitulation is complete. The **first 30 minutes after US open** is the critical observation window:

- Open holds and extends → capitulation hypothesis strengthened
- Open spikes then fades → continued base-building, stay defensive
